## Instructions

### Step 1: Preparation

**Gather your equipment and safety gear.** Put on goggles, chemical-resistant gloves, and long sleeves. Work in a well-ventilated area. Prepare your mold and line it if needed. Have all ingredients pre-measured and ready.

### Step 2: Lye Liquid

**Prepare the lye solution.** Wearing safety gear (goggles, gloves), slowly add the lye to your liquid(s) ‚ÄĒ *never* the other way around. Stir until dissolved and set aside to cool.

- Water: 203.9 g
- NaOH: 101.9 g
- Sodium Lactate: 6.1 g

### Step 3: Oil Phase

**Prepare the oils.** Weigh and combine your oils. Melt any solid oils/butters gently, then allow the oil blend to cool to your target temperature (typically 100‚Äď110 ¬įF / 38‚Äď43 ¬įC).

- Olive Oil: 500.0 g
- Coconut Oil (76¬į): 200.0 g
- Shea Butter: 100.0 g

### Step 4: Light Trace

**Combine & reach light trace.** When both lye solution and oils are at temperature, pour the lye solution into the oils and blend with a stick blender until light trace. Add the following ingredients:

- Kaolin Clay: 8.0 g
- Lavender: 24.0 g

### Step 5: Cure

Insulate the mold for 24‚Äď48 hours, then unmold and cut into bars. Cure on a rack in a cool, dry place with good airflow for **4‚Äď6 weeks** before use.
