# 🧼 Mike's Hot Process Recipe

Therapeutic hot process recipe formulated for severely dry and damaged skin. Features high conditioning (58%) and low cleansing (12%) to avoid stripping the skin's protective barrier.

**Key healing ingredients:**
- **Lanolin (30g)** - Biomimetic barrier repair, closest to human sebum
- **Shea butter (53g)** - Anti-inflammatory, vitamin-rich superfat
- **Honey (22g)** - Humectant and natural antibacterial properties
- **Salt, sodium citrate, sodium lactate** - Hardness, chelation, and DOS prevention

Gentle enough for compromised skin while maintaining good hardness and longevity.

## Instructions

### Step 1: Preparation

**Gather your equipment and safety gear.** Put on goggles, chemical-resistant gloves, and long sleeves. Work in a well-ventilated area. Prepare your mold and line it if needed. Have all ingredients pre-measured and ready.

### Step 2: Lye Liquid

**Prepare the lye solution.** Wearing safety gear (goggles, gloves), slowly add the lye to your liquid(s) — *never* the other way around. Stir until dissolved and set aside to cool.

- Water: 288.5 g
- NaOH: 144.2 g
- Salt: 11.0 g
- Sodium Citrate: 22.0 g
- Sodium Lactate: 33.0 g

### Step 3: Oil Phase

**Prepare the oils.** Weigh and combine your oils. Melt any solid oils/butters gently, then allow the oil blend to cool to your target temperature (typically 100–110 °F / 38–43 °C).

- Almond Oil, sweet: 50.0 g
- Olive Oil: 200.0 g
- Olive Oil pomace: 150.0 g
- Castor Oil: 50.0 g
- Coconut Oil, 76 deg: 180.0 g
- Beeswax: 5.0 g
- Cocoa Butter: 50.0 g
- Palm Oil: 235.0 g
- Shea Butter: 80.0 g

### Step 4: Light Trace

**Combine & reach light trace.** When both lye solution and oils are at temperature, pour the lye solution into the oils and blend with a stick blender until light trace.


### Step 5: Post Cook / After Gel

**Post-Cook / After Gel.** (For Hot Process) After the cook is complete and temperature drops below flashpoints, stir in:

- Honey: 22.0 g
- Lavender: 11.0 g
- Shea Butter: 53.0 g (Superfat Phase)
- Castor Oil: 12.0 g (Superfat Phase)
- Olive Oil: 5.0 g (Superfat Phase)
- Lanolin liquid Wax: 30.0 g (Superfat Phase)

### Step 6: Cure

Insulate the mold for 24–48 hours, then unmold and cut into bars. Cure on a rack in a cool, dry place with good airflow for **4–6 weeks** before use.

## Notes

Hot process with post-cook superfat phase. Base oils fully saponified, superfat oils (100g) added after cook to preserve conditioning properties.
